Biography

Charlotte Jolly is a clarinet and saxophone player and vocalist based in Suffolk. She is musically diverse and in demand as a chamber musician, singer, song-writer and avid improviser. Since graduating from Trinity Laban in 2009 with a first class degree in the clarinet, she has had a busy performance career performing with Lontano Ensemble, Perhaps Contraption, Hetty and the Jazzato Band, Tafahum Ensemble, Wilderness Orchestra, Brighton Philharmonic, the New Music Players, and as a session musician on world-wide tours, radio performances and festivals with groups including These New Puritans, Ebony Bones and the Sparks at venues ranging from Fuji Rock Festival to Glyndebourne.
